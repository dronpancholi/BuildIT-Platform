## Survey Design (6‑question Likert scale, 1–5)
| # | Question |
|---|------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1 | **Trust**: I trust the platform to correctly execute outreach and record links. |
| 2 | **Ease of Use**: The UI and workflows are intuitive and easy to operate. |
| 3 | **Speed**: Campaign creation, recommendation generation, and reporting are fast enough for my daily work. |
| 4 | **Recommendation Quality**: The recommendations are relevant and actionable. |
| 5 | **Reporting Quality**: Reports give me the data I need in a clear format. |
| 6 | **Overall Satisfaction**: I would recommend this platform to other SEO teams. |

*Responses are recorded on a 1–5 scale (1=Strongly Disagree, 5=Strongly Agree).*
